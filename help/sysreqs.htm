SYSTEM REQUIREMENTS

Penguins!™ requires:

  • PC Only

  • Pentium III, 800 MHz or better

  • Windows® 2000 / XP and above

  • 256 MB System RAM

  • Internet Explorer 5.5 and above or AOL 8.0/9.0

  • Direct X 8.1 or better
